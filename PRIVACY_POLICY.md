# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

Patotas ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

Patotas is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data to external servers. All data you enter into the app, such as player names and attributes, is stored locally on your device's internal storage.

## 2. Permissions

The app does not require any special permissions (such as location, camera, or contacts) to function.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising frameworks that collect data from your device.

## 4. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, its security is subject to your device's own security settings. We recommend keeping your device updated and secured.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
